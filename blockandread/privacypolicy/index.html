Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Block&Read ("the App"), developed by Dezeo, a studio operated by Nicolas Gula ("we", "us"), handles your information. The short version: we don't collect, see, or sell your data. Ever.

1. Information we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our own servers — we have none. Your reading activity (pages read, streaks, reading time, progress), your book list, and your settings are stored locally on your device. The App has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

2. iCloud backup

If iCloud is enabled on your device, your reading data and the books you add are backed up to your private iCloud (Apple's CloudKit and iCloud Drive). This backup belongs to your Apple Account: we have no access to it — only you do. You can remove it anytime from iOS Settings → your name → iCloud.

3. Screen Time & app blocking

The App uses Apple's Screen Time (Family Controls) framework to block the apps you choose. Apple designed this system so that developers cannot see which apps you select or when you use them — the selection is an opaque token that never leaves your device. We know nothing about your app usage.

4. Book downloads

When you download a book, the App requests the file directly from public-domain libraries: Standard Ebooks (standardebooks.org) and Project Gutenberg (gutenberg.org, via the Gutendex API). Like any web request, those servers may see standard technical data (such as your IP address), governed by their own policies. We add no identifiers to those requests.

5. Purchases

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never receive or have access to your payment information. Apple's handling of your data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

6. Notifications & alarms

Daily reading reminders and alarms are scheduled locally on your device. They involve no server and send no data anywhere.

7. Deleting your data

Delete the App to remove all local data. To remove the iCloud backup, go to iOS Settings → your name → iCloud → Manage Account Storage, or disable iCloud for Block&Read.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Your rights (GDPR & others)

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information for us to access, modify, or delete. If you have any questions about your privacy, contact us at the email below.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

11. Contact

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fix Your Life ("the App"), developed by Dezeo, a studio operated by Nicolas Gula ("we", "us"), handles your information. Your privacy matters to us, and the App is designed to collect as little data as possible.

1. Information we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your data — your alarms, schedules, and custom messages — is stored locally on your device and never leaves it. The App has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

2. Purchases

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never receive or have access to your payment information. Apple's handling of your data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

3. Notifications & alarms

The App schedules alarms and notifications locally on your device to deliver your reflective messages. This information is not sent to us or to any server.

4. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

5. Your rights (GDPR & others)

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information for us to access, modify, or delete. If you have any questions about your privacy, contact us at the email below.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

7. Contact
